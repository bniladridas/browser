Handmade Rituals

Some releases of browser may begin with a pencil, a whiteboard, and a quiet morning. We sometimes map flows by hand before code is written.

Purposeful Borders

The UI attempts to be shaped by the way people might hold their devices. Rounded cards, generous spacing, and gentle contrasts aim to give the design the breathing room of craftwork.

Manual Notes

Rough notes can become a reference point. Some sections of the app and site may be reviewed manually, with handwritten annotations sometimes guiding layout tweaks so the experience may feel intentionally stitched together.

Soft Motion

Micro-interactions try to be tuned like the movement of a slow hand brush stroke - possibly deliberate, calm, and somewhat considerate of focus.
